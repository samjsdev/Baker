ABOUT ME

I’m Janannie Rangaswamy,

The creative soul behind the edible artistry you’ll find here. With a heart that’s part coder and part confectioner, I’ve embarked on a delicious journey from software engineer to cake artist.

My story began in the realm of programming and lines of code, where I honed my logical thinking and attention to detail. But the call of creativity was too strong to resist, and one day, I found myself captivated by the artistry of cake decorating. What started as a fascination quickly turned into a passion, and before I knew it, I was trading debugging sessions for fondant sculpting.
